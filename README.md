## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product popularity, shipping efficiency, and customer satisfaction for Viva Calif Store.

The dashboard was developed in Microsoft Excel using Pivot Tables, Power Query, Power Pivot, and interactive slicers to help stakeholders make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Management needs a centralized dashboard to:

- Track sales performance
- Understand customer buying behavior
- Identify top-selling products
- Monitor shipping performance
- Analyze customer satisfaction
- Discover growth opportunities

# Key Insights

## 1. Sales Trend Analysis

### Business Question
How are sales and quantities trending over time?

### Findings

- Weekly revenue fluctuates between $30K–$50K.
- Peak sales occurred around Week 10.
- Revenue and quantity sold show similar trends.
- Recent weeks indicate a slight decline in performance.

### Recommendations

- Investigate factors behind Week 10 performance.
- Replicate successful campaigns.
- Monitor seasonal demand patterns.

---

## 2. Purchase Channel Analysis

### Business Question
Which channels drive the most purchases?

### Findings

Channels analyzed:

- App
- Website
- Instagram
- Partner App
- Target.com

The App contributes the highest share of sales while Instagram contributes the least.

### Recommendations

- Increase App marketing investment.
- Improve Website conversion funnel.
- Optimize social media campaigns.

---

## 3. Purchase Frequency Analysis

### Business Question

How many items do customers purchase per order?

### Findings

| Items Purchased | Customers |
|-----------------|-----------|
| 1 | 397 |
| 2 | 540 |
| 3 | 375 |
| 4 | 246 |
| 5 | 195 |
| 6-10 | 414 |
| >10 | 233 |

Most customers purchase only 1–2 items.

### Recommendations

- Create product bundles.
- Introduce quantity discounts.
- Implement product recommendation systems.

---

## 4. Product Performance Analysis

### Business Question

Which products are most popular?

### Top Categories

1. T-Shirts
2. Jeans
3. Sneakers
4. Tank Tops
5. Bikinis

### Findings

- Apparel dominates overall sales.
- Female customers contribute the highest sales volume.
- Several categories show low demand.

### Recommendations

- Increase inventory for top-selling products.
- Review low-performing SKUs.
- Launch personalized campaigns.

---

## 5. Geographic Analysis

### Business Question

Where do customers live?

### Findings

- California generates the highest concentration of customers.
- Southern California contributes the largest volume of orders.
- Several regions remain underpenetrated.

### Recommendations

- Expand marketing in low-penetration regions.
- Use geo-targeted advertising.

---

## 6. Shipping Performance Analysis

### Business Question

How long does shipping take?

### Findings

- Majority of orders arrive within 1–3 days.
- Average delivery time is 2.3 days.
- Delayed shipments are minimal.

### Recommendations

- Maintain delivery SLA under 3 days.
- Monitor logistics bottlenecks.

---

## 7. Customer Satisfaction Analysis

### Business Question

How satisfied are customers?

### Findings

Average Rating:

⭐ 4.0 / 5.0

Most ratings fall within 4–5 stars.

### Recommendations

- Investigate low-rating reviews.
- Improve post-purchase experience.
